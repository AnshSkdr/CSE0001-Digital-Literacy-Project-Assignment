# **Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)**

## **Prevention Tips**

* Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts, combining letters, numbers, and symbols
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email and banking
* Never share your OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or company
* Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links received through messages, emails, or social media
* Download apps only from trusted sources such as the Google Play Store or App Store
* Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security updates

## **Financial / UPI Safety**

* Never approve unknown UPI collect requests; you only need to enter your PIN when sending money
* Always verify the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment
* Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown or untrusted sources

## **General Online Safety**

* Always log out when using public or shared computers
* Be careful about what you share online and avoid posting personal information publicly

---

## **Reporting Cybercrime**

If you face any cybercrime or financial fraud:

* Report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the helpline number: 1930
