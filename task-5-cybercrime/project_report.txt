While researching cybercrime, what surprised me the most was how easily people can be tricked into UPI and online payment frauds. Many scams do not involve advanced hacking but instead rely on simple psychological manipulation, such as creating urgency or pretending to be a trusted authority. I also realized how quickly money can be transferred once a person approves a request or shares sensitive details.

As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious while handling online transactions. I will make sure to always verify payment requests, avoid clicking on unknown links, and never share OTPs or personal information with anyone. This task made me more aware of the importance of digital safety and responsible online behavior, especially as digital payments are becoming a regular part of everyday life.
