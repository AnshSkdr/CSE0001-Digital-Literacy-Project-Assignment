📱 Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist
✅ Do’s
✔️ Think before you post
✔️ Keep your profile respectful and professional
✔️ Verify information before sharing
✔️ Use strong passwords and privacy settings
✔️ Be respectful in comments and discussions
❌ Don’ts
❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information
❌ Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
❌ Don’t spread fake news or misinformation
❌ Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling
❌ Don’t overshare your daily activities publicly
