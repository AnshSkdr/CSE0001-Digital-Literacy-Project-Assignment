For creating the digital literacy infographic, I used Canva as my design tool because it provides easy-to-use templates and allows quick customization of text, colors, and layout. The design presents key aspects of digital literacy in a simple and visually organized manner. It includes sections explaining what digital literacy means, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and basic email etiquette. Each section is arranged clearly so that students can quickly understand the importance of using digital technology responsibly and effectively.

The infographic mainly focuses on spreading awareness among students about how to stay safe online, use digital tools for academic purposes, and maintain a professional image on the internet. I used simple headings and short points to make the content easy to read and visually appealing.

One thing I found interesting while making this was how small design choices, like colors and layout, can make information much easier to understand. However, it was slightly difficult to fit all important information into a single page without making it look crowded.
